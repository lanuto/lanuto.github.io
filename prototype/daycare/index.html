Meet Our Founder: Shardae Hepburn

Shardae is the passionate founder and director of Achieve Milestones Childcare LLC. Originally from Kingston, Jamaica, she brings a rich cultural heritage and deep appreciation for community-driven education. Growing up, she witnessed firsthand the impact that a nurturing, high-quality learning environment can have on a child's development, inspiring her to dedicate her career to early childhood education.

Shardae is an honors student, a proud member of Phi Theta Kappa Society (PTK), and serves as the president of her college's Early Childhood Education Club. In these roles, she actively advocates excellence in early learning and supports future educators in their professional growth. She believes that every child deserves an environment where they feel safe, valued, and encouraged to explore their potential.


Her teaching philosophy is rooted in play-based, hands-on learning that fosters social-emotional growth, cognitive development, and school readiness. Through Achieve Milestones Childcare, she is committed to providing developmentally appropriate experiences that cater to the unique needs of each child, ensuring they build a strong foundation for lifelong learning.


Shardae's journey has been shaped by inspiring mentors, such as her former professor Kellyn Jeremy, whose belief in her potential fueled her drive to succeed.


Now, as an educator and advocate, she hopes to be that same source of encouragement for young learners, helping them achieve their milestones, one step at a time.


Her vision for Achieve Milestones Childcare is simple yet profound: to create a space where children not only learn but also feel empowered, confident, and excited about their future.
